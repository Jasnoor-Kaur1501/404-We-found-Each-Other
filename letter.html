A Letter I Never Finished Writing

I’ve rewritten this page more times than I’ll admit. Not because I didn’t know what to say, but because saying it out loud felt heavier than building a website.

There were versions of us that didn’t survive. Versions that were too scared, too reactive, or too tired to keep explaining themselves.

But there was always something unfinished. Some thread that kept pulling us back.

This last year wasn’t perfect. It was quieter. Slower. And maybe that’s why it finally lasted.
