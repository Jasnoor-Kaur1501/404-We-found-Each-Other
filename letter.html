A Letter I Didn’t Know How To Write

I’m better at building things than saying things. So I built this.

We weren’t perfect. We were messy, inconsistent, emotional, and sometimes scared of each other.

But through all of it, you stayed. And even when I didn’t say it out loud, I noticed.

This last year wasn’t about fixing everything. It was about choosing each other even when things felt quiet.

I don’t take that lightly. I don’t take you lightly.
